🚨 Women Safety Analysis using EDA

📌 Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on crimes against women in India to uncover patterns, trends, and key insights from the dataset.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze different types of crimes against women
Identify top states with highest crime rates
Study trends over years
Understand relationships between crime categories

📂 Dataset
The dataset includes crime records across states and years with features such as:
State
Year
Rape
Kidnapping & Abduction (K&A)
Dowry Death (DD)
Assault on Women (AoW)
Assault on Minors (AoM)
Domestic Violence (DV)
Women Trafficking (WT)

🧹 Data Preprocessing
Removed unnecessary columns
Checked and handled missing values
Cleaned inconsistent state names
Prepared data for analysis

📊 Analysis & Visualizations
🔹 Top States with Highest Crimes
Identified top contributing states
States like Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and West Bengal show higher crime counts
🔹 Crime Distribution
Domestic Violence contributes the largest share
Followed by Assault on Women and Kidnapping
🔹 Correlation Analysis
Strong correlation between Domestic Violence and Total Crimes
Multiple crime types show positive relationships
🔹 Year-wise Trends
Overall increase in crimes over the years
Noticeable rise after 2012
Slight variations in recent years

📈 Key Insights
Domestic Violence is the most dominant crime category
Crimes have increased over time
Certain states consistently report higher cases
Crime categories are interrelated
